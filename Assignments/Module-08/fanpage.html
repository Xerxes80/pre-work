Metallica is an American heavy metal band based in San Rafael, California.
The band was formed in 1981 in Los Angeles when vocalist/guitarist James
Hetfield responded to an advertisement posted by drummer Lars Ulrich in a
local newspaper. Metallica's current line-up comprises founding members
Hetfield and Ulrich, longtime lead guitarist Kirk Hammett and bassist
Robert Trujillo. Guitarist Dave Mustaine and bassists Ron McGovney,
Cliff Burton and Jason Newsted are former members of the band.
